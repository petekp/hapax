## noun

A wrestling throw in which an opponent is lifted and arched backward, then driven to the mat, force made into geometry. A **suplex** is a controlled inversion, the body briefly becoming a lever.

> *The crowd rose as he hit the suplex, the moment hanging between lift and impact.*

## verb

Execute such a throw.

> *She suplexed her opponent cleanly, then stepped back as if nothing had happened.*

## Etymology

From French **souplesse** "suppleness, flexibility." A move brutal in effect, named for the pliancy that makes it possible.

## Related Words

- **grappling** (the wider discipline)
- **throw** (the technical category)
- **lock** (a different kind of control)
- **takedown** (general term in wrestling and MMA)
